About

Two generations. One technician per practice.

The reason our clients stay.

When you call Schein, you get a different technician every visit. They don't know your equipment, your building's access quirks, or that the chair in Op 3 has been running on a retrofitted valve since 2017.

Manhattan Dental Equipment is a sister operation to Brooklyn Dental Equipment, the family business our father started in 1985. Mike grew up in the shop, trained on every major manufacturer, and now runs the Manhattan route himself — the same route, the same tech, year after year.

We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the one call that solves the problem the first time, at half the price of a national supplier, with the documentation your compliance officer actually needs.
